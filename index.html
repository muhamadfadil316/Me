About Me

The journey begins with purpose

Before becoming the person I am today, I often found myself in situations where people underestimated me—whether it was my friends, siblings, or even my parents. Perhaps it was because, at an age where I was expected to have clear goals, I still had no direction in life.

For three years, I worked without any real purpose or meaningful experience. Life felt empty and aimless. However, one night, I came to a realization: I couldn't go on living like that. I needed change—a life with meaning, purpose, and hope.

That night marked the beginning of a new journey, and I decided to pursue higher education as my first step toward a better future. I wanted to prove that I could grow through every process, no matter how small.

Let's be friends

I'm someone you can count on—especially when others need support. I genuinely care about those around me and always try to lend a hand, believing that mutual help builds harmony within any team or group.

Whenever I'm part of a group, I make it my goal to contribute in ways that uplift everyone, while continuously working to improve myself. I'm also very open to connecting with people who are eager to grow.

I believe that personal growth and strong, supportive relationships are the foundation of meaningful and lasting friendships.
